Masuul ka tirsanaa Maamulka degmada Boondheere oo lagu dilay Degmadaasi.
Published on July 25, 2014 by Maalik Som   ·   No Comments
Muqdisho (Sh M Network)—Sida ilo deegaanka ah ay inoogu warrameen Dableey Hubeysan ayaa ku dilay Gudaha degmada Boondheere Guddoomiyihii Sport-ga Degmadaasi Jamaal Muudeey Max’ed.
Dilka Jamaal ayaa waxa uu ka dhacay Nawaaxiga Masjidka Al Raxma ee degmada Boondheere.
Kooxihii dilka Guddoomiyaha dhanka Sport-ga degmada Boondheere ka dambeeyay ayaa la xaqiijiyay in ay baxsadeen
