Salaad Cali Jeelle: ” Xildhibaanadu cidna yeysan eersan Iyagaa ammaankooda dayacay”.
Published on July 25, 2014 by Maalik Som   ·   No Comments
Muqdisho (Sh M Network)—Ex Xildhibaan Salaad Cali Jeelle oo u warramayay Idaacadda Shabelle ee Magaalada Muqdisho ayaa sheegay in Xildhibaanadu ay iyagu dayaceen ammaankooda.
Salaad Cali Jeelle ayaa tilmaamay in haddii ay la xisaabtami lahaayeen Madaxda dowladda ay ku gudbi lahaayeen war warka haatan ka heysta ammaankooda.
“ ilima aha in hadda Xildhibaanadu gudan karaan Waajibaadkooda shaqo waayo tooda ayeyba maara u la’yihiin” sidaasi waxaa yiri Salaad Cali Jeelle oo horay uga mid ahaa Xildhibaanadii KMG.
